Research

My research can be classified into the following areas:
— Computational Approaches to Linguistic Morphology: co-leading the UniMorph Project and organizing shared tasks on morphlogical reinflection (2017--)
      Frequent Collaborators: Ryan Cotterell (and his fabulous group!), David Yarowsky, Khuyagbaatar Batsuren, Ben Ambridge (child language acquisition), Salam Khalifa, Jordan Kodner, Jason Eisner, Mans Hulden, Chris Kirov, Sabrina J. Mielke, Eleanor Chodroff, Elizabeth Salesky, Omer Goldman, Reut Tsarfaty
— Computational Approaches to Linguistic Typology: co-leading SIGTYP and shared tasks on typological research (2020--).
      Frequent Collaborators: Ryan Cotterell, Edoardo M. Ponti, Johann-Mattis List
— Computational Approaches to Psychology and Quantitative Evaluation of ``Concept Creep'': analysis of diachronic changes in language.
      Frequent Collaborators: Nick Haslam, Melanie J. McGrath, Naomi Baes, Melissa Wheeler
— Technology for Field Research and Under-resourced Languages.
       Frequent Collaborators: Saliha Muradoğlu, Andrei Shcherbakov, Atul Ojha, Elena Klyachko, Oleg Serikov, Trevor Cohn, Nick Thieberger, Nick Evans
